Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does this budget calculator web application work?

Our application allows users to input their income and expenses, and then manage their budgets based on this data.

2. What does this application track?

Our application tracks your financial data including income, expenses, and savings. It also analyzes your spending by categories and helps you optimize your budget.

3. What are the benefits of budget calculation?

Budget calculation helps you understand your financial situation, keep your spending under control, and save money. With this application, setting and reaching your financial goals will be easier.

4. Is this application free?

Yes, our application is completely free and does not charge any fees.

5. On which devices can it be used?

Our website can be accessed on any browser. You can use it on your computer, tablet, or smartphone.

6. What measures do you take for privacy and security?

The privacy and security of our users are important to us. All your data is securely stored and no personal information is shared with third parties.

7. In which countries is this application available?

Our website is available worldwide. Since financial management needs are universal, our application is not limited to any specific country.

8. Do you have customer support?

Yes, if you have any questions or feedback, you can reach us through our contact form. Our customer service team will be happy to assist you.
